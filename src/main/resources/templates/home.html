About FoodShare

FoodShare is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste and addressing food insecurity by connecting businesses and individuals with surplus food to local non-profit organizations. Our mission is to ensure that no good food goes to waste while supporting communities in need.

How It Works

Donate Food

Businesses and individuals list surplus food items on the platform.

Food Collection

Our logistics team coordinates the safe and efficient collection of food.

Distribution to NGOs

Collected food is delivered to registered non-profit organizations.

Community Support

NGOs distribute food to individuals and families in need within their communities.
